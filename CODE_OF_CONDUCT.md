# Code of Conduct - AI Education Pilot

## üéØ Our Commitment

The AI Education Pilot project is dedicated to creating a **safe, inclusive, and educational environment** for all participants. We welcome educators, students, developers, and contributors from all backgrounds to collaborate in building innovative educational technology.

As a platform focused on education, we hold ourselves to high standards of conduct that reflect the values we want to promote in educational settings.

## üåü Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the AI Education Pilot community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, visible or invisible disability
- Ethnicity, nationality, race, religion, or cultural background  
- Gender identity and expression, sexual orientation
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status
- Teaching methodology, educational philosophy
- Technical skill level or programming experience

## ‚úÖ Expected Behavior

### For All Community Members

- **Be Respectful**: Treat everyone with kindness, patience, and understanding
- **Be Educational**: Focus on learning, teaching, and knowledge sharing
- **Be Collaborative**: Work together constructively toward common goals
- **Be Professional**: Maintain appropriate conduct in all interactions
- **Be Inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and support diverse perspectives

### For Educators

- **Model Good Behavior**: Demonstrate the conduct you expect from students
- **Support Learning**: Help others understand concepts and technologies
- **Share Knowledge**: Contribute educational insights and best practices
- **Protect Privacy**: Respect student confidentiality and data privacy

### For Students

- **Ask Questions**: Feel free to seek help and clarification
- **Share Experiences**: Contribute your perspective as learners
- **Respect Others**: Be considerate of different learning styles and paces
- **Stay Focused**: Keep discussions relevant to educational goals

### For Developers

- **Write Clean Code**: Follow coding standards and best practices
- **Document Well**: Provide clear documentation for educational users
- **Consider Accessibility**: Ensure features work for diverse users
- **Prioritize Security**: Protect user data and maintain platform security

## ‚ùå Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered harassment and are unacceptable:

### General Misconduct
- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, flaming, or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without consent
- Spamming, advertising, or off-topic content

### Educational Context Violations
- Sharing inappropriate content in educational settings
- Misusing AI tools for academic dishonesty
- Violating student privacy or data protection
- Promoting non-educational or harmful uses of the platform
- Circumventing security measures or access controls

### Platform Abuse
- Attempting to hack, exploit, or damage the system
- Creating fake accounts or impersonating others  
- Violating terms of service or usage policies
- Sharing API keys, credentials, or sensitive information
- Using the platform for commercial purposes without authorization

## üì¢ Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it promptly:

### How to Report
- **Email**: Send detailed reports to [ykhat1@brockport.edu]
- **GitHub Issues**: Use our [issue template](https://github.com/All-Pilot-Modules/AI-PILOT2/issues/new?template=code_of_conduct_violation.md) for public concerns
- **Direct Message**: Contact project maintainers privately when appropriate

### What to Include
- Description of the incident with specific details
- Screenshots or evidence if available
- Impact on you or the community
- Any previous related incidents
- Preferred resolution if you have suggestions

### What Happens Next
1. **Acknowledgment**: We'll respond within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We'll review the situation fairly and thoroughly  
3. **Action**: We'll take appropriate measures based on our findings
4. **Follow-up**: We'll communicate the outcome to relevant parties

## ‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take fair corrective action in response to violations.

### Possible Actions
- **Warning**: Private or public clarification of expectations
- **Temporary Restriction**: Limited participation for a specified period
- **Permanent Ban**: Removal from the project community
- **Account Suspension**: Temporary or permanent access revocation
- **Legal Action**: In cases of serious violations or illegal behavior

### Appeal Process
If you believe enforcement action was taken in error, you may:
- Email an appeal to the project maintainers
- Provide additional context or evidence
- Request a review by a different maintainer
- Escalate to institutional authorities if applicable

## üè´ Educational Institution Guidelines

For users representing educational institutions:

### Institutional Policies
- Follow your institution's code of conduct and IT policies
- Ensure compliance with FERPA and other privacy regulations
- Coordinate with IT departments for proper implementation
- Maintain appropriate faculty-student boundaries online

### Classroom Integration
- Establish clear guidelines for student platform usage
- Provide proper training on AI tool ethics and academic integrity
- Monitor student interactions and provide guidance
- Report any concerning behavior through institutional channels

## üåç Community Standards

### Open Source Values
- Collaborate openly and transparently
- Share knowledge and resources freely
- Give credit to original creators and contributors
- Respect intellectual property rights

### Educational Excellence  
- Prioritize student learning outcomes
- Support evidence-based teaching practices
- Promote digital literacy and responsible AI use
- Foster critical thinking about technology in education

### Innovation and Ethics
- Explore new educational technologies responsibly
- Consider the impact of AI on learning and assessment
- Protect student data and privacy
- Promote equitable access to educational technology
